# Play Guide - Ver.0.0.2

## 1. Entering the network

Every login begins in Lobby. Walk through the Main gate to begin normal play. Lobby is not part of the economy or character progression.

## 2. Main

Main is the permanent home for building, farming, storage, fishing and general survival exploration.

- Use `main`, `main_nether` and `main_end` for permanent construction.
- These dimensions contain additional structures and exploration targets.
- mcMMO levels increase through normal actions.
- EvenMoreFish adds collectible fish, rarity and selling gameplay.
- Sell fish, minerals and other configured goods for Waymark.

## 3. Resource worlds

Use `resource`, `resource_nether` and `resource_end` for destructive gathering.

- They are close to vanilla generation.
- They do not contain BetterStructures content.
- They may be regenerated, so do not build anything you want to keep.

## 4. Frontier

Frontier is a separate adventure environment reached through its portal hub.

- Frontier uses a separate local inventory.
- In Ver.0.0.2, items do not move between Main and Frontier.
- Waymark and mcMMO progression are shared.
- Frontier is expected to produce Waymark more efficiently than routine Main work.
- Frontier-specific equipment stays in Frontier.
- Death initially follows vanilla rules.

## 5. Network presentation

Global chat shows the origin server, for example `[MAIN]`, `[FRONTIER]` or `[LOBBY]`. TAB shows players across the network and may display Waymark or other placeholders after integration is validated.

## 6. Long-term progression

Later revisions plan to add:

- unlockable cross-server storage slots;
- Main equipment that can be certified for Frontier expeditions;
- Frontier achievements and Main-side memorial rewards;
- Frontier-local expanded storage;
- lighter configurable death penalties;
- custom shops and meta progression.
